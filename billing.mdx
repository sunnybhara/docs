## How billing works

NAWA uses a prepaid credit system.

- **1,000 credits = $1.00 USD**
- Buy credit packs, and each API call deducts credits from your balance
- Free and paid tiers run independently -- free key requests never consume paid credits

## Balance headers

Every API response includes balance information in headers:

| Header | Description | Example |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| `X-NAWA-Balance` | Current credit balance in USD | `12.45` |
| `X-NAWA-Balance-Warning` | Warning when balance is low | `low_balance` |
| `X-NAWA-Cache` | Cache status | `HIT` or `MISS` |
| `X-Request-Id` | Unique request identifier | `req_nw_abc123def456` |

<Note>
  The `X-NAWA-Balance-Warning` header appears when your balance drops below $5.00 (5,000 credits).
</Note>

## What happens when credits run out

When your credit balance reaches $0:

1. A **1-hour grace period** begins automatically. During this window, paid endpoints continue to work up to a maximum of **$2.00** in additional usage.
2. After the grace period expires (or $2.00 is reached), paid endpoints return `402 insufficient_credits`.
3. Free endpoints continue to work normally.
4. Free keys (`nawa_test_sk_`) are unaffected -- they use their own 100-request pool.
5. Purchase any credit pack to restore access immediately.

<Warning>
  The grace period gives you time to add credits, but it is limited to 1 hour and $2.00 of usage. Monitor your `X-NAWA-Balance` header to avoid service interruption.
</Warning>

## Auto top-up

Enterprise accounts can enable auto top-up to avoid credit exhaustion. Safeguards include:

- **24-hour cooldown** between top-up charges
- **Monthly spending cap** to prevent runaway costs
- **Anomaly detection** -- if spend exceeds 5x your average hourly rate, auto top-up pauses and you receive an alert

Contact [sales@trynawa.com](mailto:sales@trynawa.com) to enable auto top-up.

## Refund policy

Credit purchases are non-refundable once credits have been consumed. For billing issues or accidental purchases, contact [support@trynawa.com](mailto:support@trynawa.com) within 14 days of purchase for review.

Refunds for unused credits are processed to the original payment method within 5-10 business days.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do free key requests consume my paid credits?">
    No. Free keys (`nawa_test_sk_`) have their own pool of 100 lifetime requests. Paid credits are only consumed by live keys (`nawa_live_sk_`).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do credits expire?">
    No. Credits never expire. Use them at your own pace.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
    Unused credits can be refunded within 14 days. Consumed credits are non-refundable. Contact [support@trynawa.com](mailto:support@trynawa.com).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I hit a cached result?">
    Semantic cache hits are free. You are not charged when `X-NAWA-Cache` returns `HIT`.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I check my balance programmatically?">
    Every API response includes the `X-NAWA-Balance` header with your current balance in USD. You can also call `GET /v1/usage` to get a full balance breakdown.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is the grace period?">
    When your balance hits $0, you get a 1-hour window with up to $2.00 of additional usage. This gives you time to purchase more credits without immediate service interruption.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
