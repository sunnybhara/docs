## How billing works

NAWA uses a prepaid credit system.

- **1,000 credits = $1.00 USD**
- Buy credit packs, and each API call deducts credits from your balance
- Free and paid tiers run independently -- free key requests never consume paid credits

## Balance headers

Every API response includes balance information in headers:

| Header | Description | Example |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| `X-NAWA-Balance` | Current credit balance in USD | `12.45` |
| `X-NAWA-Cache` | Cache status | `HIT` or `MISS` |
| `X-NAWA-Environment` | Key environment | `sandbox` or `live` |
| `X-Request-Id` | Unique request identifier | `req_nw_a1b2c3d4...` |

## Grace period

When your credit balance reaches $0, NAWA activates a temporary grace period:

- **Duration:** up to 1 hour
- **Spend limit:** up to $2 worth of requests during the grace period
- **Purpose:** prevents sudden service interruption while you purchase credits

After the grace period expires (or the $2 cap is reached), all paid endpoints return `402 Payment Required` until you add credits.

<Warning>
  The grace period activates once per balance depletion. Purchase credits promptly to avoid service interruption.
</Warning>

## Auto top-up

Configure automatic credit purchases to prevent balance depletion:

- Set a **balance threshold** (e.g., $5) and a **top-up amount** (e.g., $50)
- When your balance drops below the threshold, NAWA charges your card and adds credits
- **Monthly cap** prevents runaway spend
- **24-hour cooldown** between automatic top-ups
- **Anomaly detection** pauses auto top-up if current-hour spend exceeds 5x your average hourly spend

Configure auto top-up in the dashboard under **Settings > Billing**.

## Refund policy

Credit purchases are non-refundable once credits have been consumed. For billing issues or accidental purchases, contact [support@trynawa.com](mailto:support@trynawa.com) within 14 days of purchase for review.

Refunds for unused credits are processed to the original payment method within 5-10 business days.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do free key requests consume my paid credits?">
    No. Free keys (`nawa_test_sk_`) have their own pool of 100 lifetime requests. Paid credits are only consumed by live keys (`nawa_live_sk_`).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do credits expire?">
    No. Credits never expire. Use them at your own pace.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
    Unused credits can be refunded within 14 days. Consumed credits are non-refundable. Contact [support@trynawa.com](mailto:support@trynawa.com).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I hit a cached result?">
    Semantic cache hits are free. You are not charged when `X-NAWA-Cache` returns `HIT`.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I check my balance programmatically?">
    Every API response includes the `X-NAWA-Balance` header with your current balance in USD.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is the grace period?">
    When your balance reaches $0, you get up to 1 hour (or $2 of usage) to purchase credits before paid endpoints stop working.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
