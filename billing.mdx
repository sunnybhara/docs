## How billing works

NAWA uses a prepaid credit system.

- **1,000 credits = $1.00 USD**
- Buy credit packs, and each API call deducts credits from your balance
- Free sandbox requests never consume paid credits

## Free tier

  <Card title="Free tier (sandbox)" icon="flask">

    - No credit card required
    - Uses real AI models -- same accuracy as paid
    - Rate limit: 10 requests/minute
    - Free endpoints (health, usage, analytics, comments, feedback) do not count toward the 100-request limit
    - When exhausted: purchase credits to create a live key

## Trial

When you sign up for a NAWA account, you automatically receive a **7-day trial** with the basic plan. During the trial:

- Your rate limit is 60 requests/minute (trial tier)
- No credit card required
- Full access to all endpoints

After the trial ends, purchase credits to continue with a live key.

| Endpoint | Cost per call | Credits deducted |
|----------|--------------|------------------|
| `POST /v1/classify` | $0.006 | 6 credits |
| `POST /v1/translate` | $0.005 | 5 credits |
| `POST /v1/detect` | $0.002 | 2 credits |
| `POST /v1/moderate` | $0.004 | 4 credits |
| `POST /v1/rubric-classify` | $0.003 | 3 credits |
| `POST /v1/comments/reply` | $0.008 | 8 credits |
| `POST /v1/feedback` | **Free** | 0 credits |
| `GET /v1/comments` | **Free** | 0 credits |
| `GET /v1/analytics` | **Free** | 0 credits |
| `GET /v1/usage` | **Free** | 0 credits |
| `GET /v1/health` | **Free** | 0 credits |

<Note>
  Semantic cache hits are free. When NAWA returns a cached result (`X-NAWA-Cache: HIT`), no credits are deducted.
</Note>

## Balance alerts

NAWA sends webhook notifications when your balance reaches critical levels:

- `credits.low` -- balance is running low
- `credits.exhausted` -- balance has reached zero

Configure webhook endpoints in the [dashboard](https://trynawa.com/developers/keys) or via the [webhooks API](/api-reference/webhooks).

## Refunds

If you experience a service outage or billing error, contact [support@trynawa.com](mailto:support@trynawa.com) within 30 days. Refunds are processed back to your credit balance, and the corresponding credits are deducted.
