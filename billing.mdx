NAWA uses a prepaid credit system. **1,000 credits = $1.** Buy credit packs and each API call deducts credits from your balance.

## Credit packs

| Pack | Price | Credits | Best for |
|------|-------|---------|----------|
| **Starter** | $25 | 25,000 | Prototyping and small projects |
| **Builder** | $50 | 50,000 | Growing applications |
| **Scale** | $100 | 100,000 | Production workloads |
| **Enterprise** | $500 | 500,000 | High-volume usage |

Purchase credit packs at [trynawa.com/developers/keys](https://trynawa.com/developers/keys).

<Tip>
  Semantic cache hits (`X-NAWA-Cache: HIT`) cost **0 credits**, even on paid endpoints. Classifying the same or semantically similar text is free after the first call.
</Tip>

## Understanding credits

Every API response from a paid endpoint includes two billing fields:

| Field | Type | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| `cost_usd` | number | Cost in USD for this request |
| `credits_used` | integer | Credits deducted from your balance |

For example, a classify request returns `"cost_usd": 0.006, "credits_used": 6`.

## Free tier

Every account gets **500 free requests/month** on `/v1/rubric-classify`, no credit card required. This resets on the 1st of each month (UTC).

## Grace period

When your balance hits **0 credits**, NAWA provides a grace period of the lesser of:

- **1 hour** of continued access, or
- **2,000 credits** ($2) worth of API calls

This prevents your production traffic from immediately failing while you add credits.

<Warning>
  The grace period is a one-time buffer. Once it expires, all paid endpoints return `402 insufficient_credits` until you add credits.
</Warning>

## Auto top-up

Enable auto top-up to never run out of credits:

| Setting | Default |
|---------|---------|
| **Trigger threshold** | 5,000 credits ($5.00) |
| **Top-up amount** | 25,000 credits ($25.00) |
| **Monthly cap** | 100,000 credits ($100.00) |
| **Anomaly detection** | Enabled, pauses top-up on 5x spend spikes |

Configure auto top-up at [trynawa.com/developers/keys](https://trynawa.com/developers/keys).

<Info>
  Anomaly detection monitors your spending pattern. If your spend rate increases 5x above your trailing 7-day average, auto top-up pauses and you receive an email alert. This protects against runaway costs from misconfigured integrations.
</Info>
